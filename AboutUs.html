About Krishi Organics

Krishi Organics was founded in 2020 by Mr. Prem Shankar Tiwari and Rajiv Kumar Jha, bringing together over 25 years of expertise in the agrochemical industry. With a deep understanding of the challenges faced by modern farmers, they set out to create a company that offers innovative and sustainable solutions for crop growth, protection, and high yields.

At Krishi Organics, we are committed to providing a comprehensive range of high-quality products, including fertilizers, biofertilizers, plant growth regulators, and pesticides. Our dedication to research and development ensures that our products are at the forefront of agricultural innovation, helping farmers achieve better results with environmentally friendly practices.

Our founders' extensive experience in manufacturing all types of agrochemical products has shaped our approach to quality and customer satisfaction. We prioritize the needs of our customers and work closely with them to provide tailored solutions that meet their specific challenges. Our team of agronomists and technical experts is always available to offer guidance and support, ensuring that our customers receive the best possible service.

Krishi Organics is more than just a supplier; we are a partner in your agricultural success. Our mission is to empower farmers with the tools and knowledge they need to grow healthier, more productive crops while promoting a sustainable future for agriculture. Join us on this journey and experience the difference that Krishi Organics can make for your farm.
