GANNA
Bio Nutrient

Technical Name: Amino Acid + Humic Acid


GANNA Contains Humic Acid which is extracted from decomposed organic matter, In addition, it also contains Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Sulphur, Calcium, Magnesium, Copper, Zinc and other micro nutrients, Ganna accelerates germination by stimulating enhanced respiration, which leads to better root and shoot growth in seedlings. Plant grown in acidic and aalcareous soils have problem of up- take of phosphorus from soil. This problem is solved by chelating action of Ganna. Contents of Ganna can be broadly groups to Humic group and fulvic group which are two most important products of microbial degradation of plant and animal debris in the oil.


Use/Acre: 1ltr


Crop: Sugarcane, Fruits, Flowers and Vegetables


Benefits: Increases water holding capacity of soil, Controls leaching of plant nutrients by chelating action.


Packaging: 100ml, 250ml, 500ml, 1ltr
