GREEN GOLD
Bio Nutrient

Technical Name: Chelated Micronutrients with EDTAIron (Fe), Zinc (Zn), Manganese (Mn), Copper (Cu)


Green GOLD is a chelated blend of essential micronutrients, including Magnesium (Mg), Sulphur (S), Iron (Se), Manganese (Mn), Zinc (Zn), Copper (Cu), Boron (B), and Molybdenum (Mo), among others. Green GOLD significantly enhances vegetative growth, floral initiation, fruit and grain formation, as well as enzyme activation, cell respiration, cell division, and nitrogen fixation. When applied at the recommended dosage, Green GOLD promotes healthier crops, increases resistance to diseases and pests, and improves both the quality and quantity of crop yields.


Use/Acre: Foliar Spray: 250ml


Crop: Paddy, Wheat, Mentha, Potato, Bhindi, Pea, Other Crops


Benefits: Improved vegetative growth, enhanced flowering, increased flower-to-fruit conversion, and reduced flower and fruit drop.


Packaging: 100ml, 250ml, 500ml, 1ltr
