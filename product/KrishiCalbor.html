KRISHI CALBOR PLUS
Bio Nutrient

Technical Name:Calcium + Boron


Krishi Calbor Plus is a combination of Amino chelated Calcium and Boron (Ca. 30% & B. 5%) which are important for physiological and biochemical processes of the plant. It is readily soluble in water. Krishi Calbor Plus protects crops from Deficiencies such as Necrosis at the tips and margins of young leaves, bulb abnormalities and physiological flower drop. Deformation of affected leaves, Highly branched, short, brown root systems Severe, stunted growth, and general chlorisis. Size, shapes and deformities of fruits. Increase recovery (sugar content) in sugarcane.


Use/Acre: 1ltr


Crop: Paddy, Wheat, Mentha, Potato, Bhindi, Pea, Other Crops


Benefits: Zinc deficiency control, Develop disease resistance


Packaging: 100ml, 250ml, 500ml, 1ltr
