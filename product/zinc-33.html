KRISHAK ZINC-33
Bio Nutrient

Technical Name: Zinc Sulphate 33%


KRISHAK ZINC-33 provides Zinc, a vital micronutrient needed for the survival of the plant. Zinc is an essential part of Auxins and is a key element in Nitrogen Metabolism in plants. It is also essential for disease resistance. Deficiency of Zinc leads to stunted growth and causes Khaira disease in paddy. KRISHAK ZINC-33 also provides Sulphur, another element vital for plant growth.


Use/Acre: Foliar Spray: 250 gm, Soil Application: 4 - 5kg


Crop: Paddy, Wheat, Mentha, Potato, Bhindi, Pea, Other Crops


Benefits: Zinc deficiency control, Develop disease resistance


Packaging: 250gm
